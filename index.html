Objective 01
Non-Linear FEA Model
Develop a non-linear FEA model for PLA-based Octet Truss wireframe wings in ANSYS to predict structural deformation under aerodynamic loads at 10–15 km altitude.
Objective 02
Multi-Objective Topology Optimization
Build a Python framework that minimizes airframe mass while maximizing solar cell surface area using evolutionary optimization algorithms.
Objective 03
Energy-Aware Coupling Simulation
Simulate energy-aware coupling to quantify how wing deformation affects solar incidence angle and net daily energy collection efficiency.
